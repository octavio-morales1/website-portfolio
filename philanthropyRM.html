My Philanthropy Work

Philanthropy

Trunk or Treat

I participated in a Trunk or Treat community event, partnering with Sigma Lambda Upsilon, Señoritas Latinas Unidas Sorority, Inc. to hand out candy to local kids. I helped with setup, decorations, and candy distribution to create a safe and festive Halloween experience for families.

Red Cards Community Service

I contributed to the Red Cards initiative by preparing and delivering resource cards to inform the community of their rights and different resources available.

Stevens Institute of Technology MLK Week of Service

In honor of Dr. Martin Luther King Jr., I participated in campus and community service projects, including donation drives and neighborhood clean-ups. I supported volunteer coordination and day-of logistics to maximize turnout and impact.

Brown and Gold Christmas 2024

I joined a campus-wide effort to wrap and organize holiday gifts for families in partnership with the Hoboken Housing Authority. Our team prepared hundreds of presents and coordinated distribution to ensure local children received support during the holidays.

Midnight Run

I volunteered alongside the Hermanas of Lambda Pi Chi Sorority, Inc. to support Midnight Run, distributing clothing, hygiene kits, and food to individuals experiencing homelessness. I assisted with donation sorting, packing, and the late-evening outreach route.

One Day Hoboken 2024

I participated in this year's One Day Hoboken for the Hoboken Housing Authority, which aims to clean up different parts of Hoboken, making a safer environment for everyone. I contributed towards cleaning and trash by the pier and nearby homes, encouraging others to join in on the cleaning!

Suicide Prevention Walk 2024

I participated with the people of Hoboken in this year’s March Out of Darkness walk, which aims to promote suicide prevention and awareness. By taking part in the event, we supported efforts to address the alarming rate of suicide and raised awareness about mental health issues.

SHPE Beach Sweeps 2024

As a member of SHPE, I participated in our annual Beach Sweep event organized in collaboration with Clean Ocean Action. I contributed to the event by assisting with logistics and volunteer coordination, helping ensure a successful cleanup of local beaches. My involvement also included promoting the event, highlighting its impact on environmental protection.

Hoboken Homeless Shelter

I volunteered with the Hermanos of the Alpha Alpha chapter of La Unidad Latina, Lambda Upsilon Lambda Fraternity Inc. at the Hoboken Homeless Shelter, where we assisted in preparing and serving dinner. We provided over 50 meals to those in need, making a significant impact on the community and demonstrating our commitment to supporting the less fortunate.

Brown and Gold Christmas

I participated in a collaborative community event where numerous organizations came together to wrap presents for the Hoboken Housing Authority. I assisted in preparing over 330 gifts and contributed to raising more than $1,200 in funds. The gifts were organized for distribution at the HHA Holiday Celebration, highlighting our collective effort in giving back to the community.
