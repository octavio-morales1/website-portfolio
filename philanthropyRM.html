My Philanthropy Work

Philanthropy

Hoboken Homeless Shelter

I volunteered with the Hermanos of the Alpha Alpha chapter of La Unidad Latina, Lambda Upsilon Lambda Fraternity Inc. at the Hoboken Homeless Shelter, where we assisted in preparing and serving dinner. We provided over 50 meals to those in need, making a significant impact on the community and demonstrating our commitment to supporting the less fortunate.

Brown and Gold Christmas

I participated in a collaborative community event where numerous organizations came together to wrap presents for the Hoboken Housing Authority. I assisted in preparing over 330 gifts and contributed to raising more than $1,200 in funds. The gifts were organized for distribution at the HHA Holiday Celebration, highlighting our collective effort in giving back to the community.

SHPE Beach Sweeps

As a member of SHPE, I participated in our annual Beach Sweep event organized in collaboration with Clean Ocean Action. I contributed to the event by assisting with logistics and volunteer coordination, helping ensure a successful cleanup of local beaches. My involvement also included promoting the event, highlighting its impact on environmental protection.
